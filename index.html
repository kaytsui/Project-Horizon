Project Horizon

Our Mission

Project Horizon is a student run group at UCLA that strives to learn and share the wisdom and experience of our campus' professors by asking thought provoking questions. Inspired by the UCLA School of the Arts and Architecture's 10 Questions series, Project Horizon hopes to bridge North and South Campus, the Humanities with STEM. By providing a platform to learn about expert prespectives from every department on campus, Project Horizon adds to the interdisciplinary forums at UCLA that hopes to broaden points of view and expand ways of thinking.
